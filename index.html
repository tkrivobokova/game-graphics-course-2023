Game Graphics Course 2023

Mandatory Home Assignment, deadline 19.02.2024

Create a demo based on examples, which would combine several rendering algorithms together.
Your work must be pushed to your forked github repository. It will be 50% of your result mark.
Deadline 19.02.2024

Your demo must include

  • Your own 3D model(s)
  • Your own textures
  • Any lighting model (Phong, Blinn-Phong, Lambert, Cook Torrance)
  • Reflection (Planar or Cubemap)
  • Shadows (Shadowmap)
  • Any postprocessing effect (optional)

Use assignment.js as a boilerplate template: Assignment
